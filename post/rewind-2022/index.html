re:Wind 2022 - A stepping stone, not a milestone.

Summary

  • Every year in December, I create a list of my annual achievements vs. what I could have done better, self-retrospective. This helps me to establish my achievable goals and prevent myself from frustration.

  • 2021 was an average year with the pandemic & work from home. I focused on most of the learning and established a constant routine between work, learning & community contribution.

  • So the goals for 2022 were more focused on bringing the routine to the next level. Let me share my year 2022 in numbers.

    • 🧾 5 AWS Certifications
    • 🎤 Presented at AWS re:Invent 2022 & AWS Summit India 2022
    • 👥 Organised AWS Usergroup India Community Day Virtual Edition
    • 🎬 Hosted close to 28 talks/meetups
    • 🎟️ Hosted 17 weeks of AWS Solution Architect Associate Certification Bootcamp & 3 hands-on Workshops
    • 🎙 Speaker at 21 sessions & 3 AWS Community Days (India, Nashik & SriLanka)
    • 🖊️ Close to 10 blog posts & 2 APN blogs
    • 🏅 Recognised as @awscloud Community Hero, @awscloud APAC Earn Trust, @awscloud Ambassador of the year, MultiCloud4u MVP, 6 R&R from Quantiphi

Highlights from 2022

  • Top Contributions

    • AWS Solution Architect Associate Certification Bootcamp -

      • In Feb 2022, we successfully completed the AWS SAA Bootcamp, a project that took roughly 5 months from conceptualizing to delivering.
      • So far, we have 10k+ views, and the feedback has been overwhelmingly positive. We are grateful for the support and enthusiasm of the Bootcamp audience.
      • Overall, this has been a challenging but rewarding experience.

    • AWS Summit India 2022

      • In May 2022, I got a chance to deliver a session - Build a data lake with AWS Lake Formation, at AWS Summit India 2022.
      • This was my first big conference after the pandemic, and overall, it was a different experience to visit the AWS office and record the session.
      • I’m thankful to Ridhima Kapoor(Developer Marketing Manager, AWS) and Rohini Gaonkar(Senior Developer Advocate, AWS) for allowing me to deliver this session.

    • AWS re:Invent 2022

      • Finally, I got a chance to deliver a breakout session at AWS re:Invent - Reinvent your business with an AWS modern data strategy. It was one of my dream to deliver a session on such a global stage facing 100s people.
      • It was a different experience, like multiple rounds of the technical check, speaker coaching, etc. But all these hectic runs from one room to another in Vegas during the event will help me improve my public speaking.
      • I’m thankful to Lalit Khatter(PSA, AWS Ambassador India Lead, AWS) and Jia Masoud(Sr. Program and Operations Manager, AWS) for allowing me to deliver this session & my wonderful co-speakers Sudhir Gupta(Principal Partner Solutions Architect, AWS) and Veena Vasudevan(Senior Partner Solutions Architect, AWS).

    • AWS Usergroup India Community Day Virtual Edition

      • AWS Usergroup India Community Day Virtual Edition - one of the mainstream events where event logistics and content is planned, sourced and delivered by community leaders.
      • I got an opportunity to be one of the organizers and lead the speaker management team.
      • This event was “by the community, for the community,” with good peer-to-peer learning for the management team.

  • Rewards & Recognition

    • AWS Heroes program recognizes a vibrant, worldwide group of AWS experts whose enthusiasm for knowledge-sharing has a tangible impact on the community.

    • AWS Ambassador of 2022 is honored to recognize the Ambassadors who provide exemplary contributions to the AWS community.

    • AWS APAC Earn Trust is honored to an individual who earned Trust within their community and inspired others with their active contributions.

    • MultiCloud4u MVP is awarded to an individual who volunteers for MultiCloud4u, a community of more than 10000 technologists & supports making the Tech community stronger and transitioning towards the technology and integrations of the 5th Industrial revolution.

  • Memory Lane

Learning from 2022

  • Have friends who not only support but criticize to get the best out of you 🤐
  • Understanding the notions of time is essential, as two things will define you, your patience when you have nothing and your attitude when you have everything.
  • Focusing on your physical and mental health is essential, so yoga, music, and timeouts/breaks are very important.
  • Below are two quotes that help to shift my gears in tough times.
    • Work Hard & Dream Big. 👨‍💻 🚀 (my mobile screensaver & laptop wallpaper are also the same 😛)
    • Never become so much of an expert that you stop gaining expertise. View life as a continuous learning experience.

Goodbye, 2022!

  • Overall it has been an exciting year. I’m glad I could finally catch up with friends in-person at AWS re:Invent 2022 after so long.
  • I am grateful for the opportunity to share my knowledge across different communities and for the support I have received from everyone.
  • As the year closes, I hope you have a chance to relax and spend time with your friends & family. Wishing you a happy holiday season and a prosperous 2023!
